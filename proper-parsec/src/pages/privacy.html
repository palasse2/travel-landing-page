Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need, use it only for what we say, and never sell it. Here's exactly how that works.

01 — Collection

Information We Collect

When you purchase a product, we collect the information necessary to process your order — your name, email address, and payment details. Payment processing is handled entirely by Gumroad; we never store your card information directly.

If you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us by email, we collect the information you provide in that message — your name and email address, and whatever you choose to write to us.

We collect the minimum necessary. We don't ask for information we don't need, and we don't retain it longer than required.

02 — Use

How We Use It

Your information is used for three things: fulfilling your order, sending you updates directly related to your purchase, and responding to any support requests you send us.

If you've opted into our newsletter, we may send you travel tips, guide updates, or occasional offers. You can unsubscribe at any time — every email we send includes a clearly visible unsubscribe link.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it for any purpose beyond what is described here.

03 — Cookies

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to understand how visitors use the site — which pages are visited, how long people spend on them, and where they arrive from. This helps us improve the site and the products we offer.

We use standard analytics tools for this. No personally identifiable information is attached to cookie data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some parts of the site may not function fully without them.

04 — Third Parties

Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to run the business: Gumroad for payments and product delivery, and standard email providers for support correspondence. Each of these operates under their own privacy policy and data protection standards.

We only work with third-party providers whose data handling practices meet a standard we're comfortable with. We do not share your data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

05 — Security

Data Security

All data transmitted through our site is encrypted via HTTPS. Payment processing runs entirely through Gumroad's secure infrastructure — your financial information never passes through our servers.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect the data we do hold, no system is entirely immune to risk. If we ever become aware of a breach affecting your data, we will inform you promptly and take immediate corrective action.

06 — Your Rights

Your Rights

You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you, to request a copy of it, and to request that we correct or delete it. You can exercise any of these rights by emailing us directly at support@travelreadz.com.

We will respond to data requests within 30 days. If your request requires more time, we'll let you know. There is no charge for making a data request.

To unsubscribe from emails, simply click the unsubscribe link in any message we've sent you. To request deletion of your data, email us directly.

07 — Updates

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, if we add a new service or if applicable law changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date at the top.

We won't make material changes to how we use your data without giving you clear notice. If a significant change affects how your information is handled, we'll inform existing customers directly by email.
