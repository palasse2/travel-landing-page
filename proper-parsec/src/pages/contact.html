Common Questions

Things people ask us most

If your question is here, you have your answer. If it's not, use the form above.

Q —
When do I get access after purchasing?
Immediately. You'll receive a download link by email the moment your payment clears. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
Q —
I can't access my purchase. What do I do?
Email us at support@travelreadz.com with your order number. We'll sort it within 24 hours — and if the issue is on our end, we'll fix it immediately.
Q —
Can I get personal advice for my specific trip?
Yes — that's exactly what the Private Travel Assistance bonus is for. Once you purchase the guide, you get direct access to one of our team for real-time trip questions.
Q —
Do you work with travel bloggers or affiliates?
We do, selectively. We look for people who genuinely cover Morocco, not those just chasing a commission. Use the form above — select "Partnership" and introduce yourself properly.
Q —
Is the guide updated regularly?
Yes. We review and update the guide periodically to reflect price changes, new recommendations, and seasonal information. Buyers always get the latest version.
Q —
What if the guide isn't right for me?
We're confident you'll find it valuable — but if you have a genuine concern after purchasing, reach out. We handle every case individually and we're not interested in unhappy customers.
